# Research & Requirements

## Goal

Understand the problem space and gather all relevant context before designing a solution.

## Findings

- Users need to visualize markdown-based workflows
- Files change frequently as agents write to them — live reload is essential
- Both a kanban-style workflow view and a flat wiki view are needed
